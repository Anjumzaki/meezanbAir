    SINGAPORE

    Singapore is the country which tourism and extraordinary touristic offers renew day by day and so it doesn’t come strange to hear that just in 2017. more than 17 million of people took tour over this place. Once a British colonial trading point, today it is a thriving global financial hub and described as one of Asia’s economic “tigers”.

    With tall buildings, bling and sparkles and really fast transport system this place deserves probably top spot on your ultimate bucket list.

    Its chic streets and shopping malls as well as the all luxury hotels and nice dining places will surely not leave you indifferent but Singapore is something more to real wanderers and natural lovers. And for the nation itself it is important to keep greenery so most of the money is going for investment of project “City in a Garden”, that is a dream of having green skyscrapers. The big advantage when traveling to Singapore is the fact that one of official languages is English and its easy to get any information and help throughout country.

    While in Singapore necessarily explore “Gardens by the Bay”, “Underwater World”, “Singapore Flyer”, “Universal Studio Theme Park” and much more!
