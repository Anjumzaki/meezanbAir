    MALAYSIA

    Malaysia is a country located in South-east Asia occupying the Malay peninsula and island of Borneo. Populated by a blend of Malays, Chinese, Indians and indigenous groups, Malaysia boasts a rich cultural heritage, from a huge variety of annual festivals and wonderful cuisines, to traditional architecture and rural crafts.

Malaysia can be proud of its special marine life, richness of sea species there and fact of having world's most beautiful corals.

    Famous slogan "Malaysia, truly Asia" describes shortly the whole diversity of this unique country. Kuala Lumpur, its capital is home nearly to 2 million inhabitants and it offers lively life, reminding a bit on famous western metropolises. And while Petronas Twin Towers,most famous landmark of Kuala Lumpur may give you a feeling of being in New York places such Pulau Gemia, a private island resort will offer you moments of piece and calm evenings near lovely sand beaches that you will wish to spend your whole journey just there.
