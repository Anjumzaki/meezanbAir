    DUBAI - The City of Life

    The world's most expensive, outlandish, vibrant, completely new and unique cosmopolitan city, or in one word simply Dubai. The only place in the world that owns more than 200 man-made islands in the shape of different countries, city with the tallest building ever made and only point where you can choose to stay guest in one of numerous 7-star hotels. Yes, you've heard good. This city, which can actually be a synonym for luxury is relatively new, with history just a bit longer than 150 years but which developed with unbelievable high speed especially after 1980's. Before that, Dubai was mainly just an open desert.

    Dubai presents one of seven emirates that when combined make up UAE. Except for being famous for its Burj Khalifa and exclusive shopping malls Dubai in recent years, mostly because of its strategic location in Arabian peninsula and brand new infrastructure is also becoming one of leading business hubs. This stunning city with excellent urban organizing, dozens of indoor and outdoor activities including rides to desert and safari and also undoubtedly impressive architecture and newest technologies is definitely a perfect travel destination for anybody.
