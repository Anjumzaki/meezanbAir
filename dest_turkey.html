    ISTANBUL

    Centuries ago Napoleon gave an interesting and quite famous line that still describes Istanbul probably in most beautiful way: "If the World would be one country, Istanbul would be its capital."

    The fact that more than 7 million tourists visit this city every year is just fascinating. The cultural places such Hagia Sophia, Basilica Cistern, Blue Mosque or magnificent palaces such Dolmabahçe Sarayı or Topkapı Sarayı, make tourists from Far East or even South America to dare to begin the long journey into unknown land.

    Perfect place for long walks, tea time by seashore or great shopping of both unique souvenirs as well as the most expensive brands and all for really acceptable prices.

    Much different than other European capitals, Istanbul is the city that has heart and the soul, beautiful spirit and incredibly nice and polite hosts. It doesn't matter if you are travelling there first or tenth time the feeling is always the same. If you haven't already, now it's the right time to put Istanbul to your bucket list.

    Famous Places

    • Hagia Sophia, Blue Mosque
    • Dolmabahce Sarayi, Topkapi Saray
    • Kız Kulesi (Maiden Tower)
    • Galata Tower
    • Grand Bazaar
    • Hippodrome
    • Istanbul Modern Museum
