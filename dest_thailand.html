    THAILAND

    Thailand is one of the countries that can answer a great variety of tourist lifestyles and needs. Most likely the first destination to visit in South-East Asia, Thailand contains a vast array of different natural attractions. Plateu of mountains, pure islands, stunning coral reefs and innocuous sea life are there just to stare and enjoy.

    Experience the famous thai massage and make your travel focus on real, fresh thai food as we know how much thai food is famous and renowned around the globe. Except vibrant Bangkok definitely let old and fascinating city of Chiang Mai be on your travel route. Important thing to know when visiting Thailand is that official rainy season lasts from July till October so that probably is not the best time to travel to this exotic country. Full Moon party in Koh Phangan, a part of famous festival in this city is visited every year with near 40.000 party lovers and is one of top recommendations in every travel guide for Thailand. Thailand with its unique energy and well developed nightlife is also a great place to make new friends and enjoy with random people.
